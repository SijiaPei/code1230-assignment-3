Introduction



The numbers show there were only 86.5 men to every 100 women in the latest census results released by the Australian Bureau of Statistics on Monday. The figures reveal while women outnumber men 19,064 to 18,343 across all ages. [ The West Australian. (2018)] In some rural areas, the number of women is significantly higher than that of men. As time goes on, the number of men and women is getting worse. If we do not attach importance to this problem, the number of men and women will be seriously unbalanced in the future.

Farm women contribute about $8 billion annually to the rural economy through free housework.
